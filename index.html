2.Skins
Skins is the third studio album by American rapper and singer XXXTentacion, released on December 7, 2018. It is one of the projects XXXTentacion was working on before his death, and was released posthumously. The album's features include Travis Barker of Blink-182 and Kanye West.

3.Revenge
Revenge is the second mixtape by American rapper and singer XXXTentacion, released on May 16, 2017, by Empire Distribution. It consists of eight previously released songs that were available for streaming on X's SoundCloud.
<>

4.Bad Vibes
Bad Vibes Forever is the fourth and final studio album by American rapper and singer XXXTentacion. It is his second posthumous solo album following his death on June 18, 2018.
